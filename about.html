About Me

Educational Background I am currently studying Digital Business and Innovation in Tokyo International University. This website is made as part of my Introduction to Informatics and Computing Course.

Ethnic Background I am from the Philippines, a country in Southeast Asia. To be more specific, I am from Benguet, Cordillera. It is full of mountain ranges hencewhy they call us "Igorots" menaing "People of the Mountain"

What brought me to Japan? I am currently in Japan to study. I never thought of studying abroad which is why I am extremely grateful to be able to do so through a scholarship.

How is life in Japan so far? It's been a year but honestly I am still adjusting to life here. Compared to where I'm from, Japan is a pretty much like a beehive with a lot of bustling bees. There are indeed endless opportunities but people need to be careful before those 'opportunities' end them first. Hence, I really suggest taking a breather once in a while, go to a body of water and have some bread. You'll realise being a duck is quite enjoyable.
